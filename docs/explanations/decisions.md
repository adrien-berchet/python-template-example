# Architecture Decision Records

This directory contains Architecture Decision Records (ADRs) — lightweight documents
that capture significant design decisions, their context, and consequences.

## Format

```markdown
## ADR-NNN: Title

**Status**: Proposed | Accepted | Deprecated | Superseded by ADR-NNN

**Context**: What situation or constraint forced this decision?

**Decision**: What was decided?

**Consequences**: What are the results — both positive and negative?
```
